Featured Projects
Projects
Loans & Risk Management Dashboard
  • Tools: Power BI, Excel.
  • Problem: Disjointed risk data and a lack of visibility into Non-Performing Loans (NPLs) across branches and risk buckets.
  • Impact: Unified loan portfolios into a strategic monitoring tool — identified high-risk concentration (3.55% Default Rate) in specific zones and streamlined NPL tracking for a $196M total remaining balance.
  • Risk Metrics: Monitoring critical KPIs like Default Rate +90% and NPLs to mitigate financial exposure.
  • Geographic Analysis: Visualizing the concentration of the $196.15M remaining balance across Cairo, Alexandria, and Giza for localized risk strategies.
  • Risk Bucketing: Categorizing loans (Low, Medium, High Risk) to prioritize collection efforts and resource allocation.
  • Trend Analysis: Tracking the correlation between loan counts and remaining balances by quarter to forecast liquidity.
Healthcare Data Analytics Dashboard
  • Tools: Power BI, SQL.
  • Problem: Dispersed hospital records and difficulty in tracking revenue performance against patient care metrics across different departments and cities.
  • Impact: Unified hospital operations for 1.5K visits into a single reporting hub — analyzed 12.03M in total revenue and optimized resource allocation by identifying top-performing specialties (Dentistry, Ophthalmology) and tracking the average treatment cost per patient (8.02K).
  • Specialty Performance: Tracking revenue distribution across 10+ medical departments to identify growth opportunities.
  • Financial Health: Monitoring Revenue by Payment Method (Insurance vs. Cash) and doctor-specific revenue generation.
  • Geographic Reach: Visualizing patient distribution across Egypt and Jordan to optimize expansion and service delivery.
  • Care Metrics: Correlating revenue with operational KPIs like Consultation Rates (28.20%) and Average Treatment Days (3.93) to ensure quality of care.
Retail Sales Performance Dashboard
  • Tools: Advanced Excel, Power Query, Pivot Tables.
  • Problem: Complex sales data across multiple categories (Grocery, Nuts, Oils) and payment methods with no clear trend visibility.
  • Impact: Built a comprehensive KPI tracker for 1.1M+ in sales — enabled category-level performance analysis and identified seasonal trends, leading to better inventory management and a 41.66% profit margin optimization.
  • Financial Oversight: Tracking Total Sales, Costs, and Profit across 10,000 orders with real-time margin analysis (P_M).
  • Category Deep-Dive: Analyzing performance across product lines (Grocery leads with 237k) to optimize stock levels.
  • Payment Insights: Visualizing sales by payment channel (Cash, Visa, Mada) to understand customer behavior.
  • Geographic Distribution: Comparing Cairo vs. Giza market share to direct regional marketing efforts.
  • Trend Forecasting: Using a time-series line chart with a trendline to predict sales growth and seasonal spikes.
Banking Portfolio & Accounts Analytics
  • Tools: Power BI, SQL, Python .
  • Problem: Fragmented banking data across different systems, making it difficult to track account growth and interest liabilities.
  • Impact: Developed a unified financial cockpit managing $299M in total balances — providing deep visibility into account segmentation and interest trends for 1.2K+ customers, enabling more accurate liquidity forecasting.
  • Data Engineering (SQL): Cleaned and joined complex banking tables to create a robust data model for high-performance reporting.
